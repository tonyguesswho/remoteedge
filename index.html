Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does RemoteEdge make money?

We're compensated by the companies when we successfully match them with qualified candidates. You'll never be charged or asked for payment information — our service is completely on the employer side.

How long does the matching process take?

Most applicants hear back within 2–3 business days. Our team carefully reviews each application to find the best fit. Some high-demand roles may match even faster.

What types of remote jobs are available?

We offer a wide range of remote positions including AI Training & Data Annotation, Virtual Assistant, Customer Support, Content & Writing, Sales & Marketing, and Data Entry & Research. Pay ranges from $15 to $75+ per hour depending on the role and experience level.

Do I need prior experience?

Not necessarily. Many of our positions are open to beginners, including students. We have entry-level roles like data entry, customer support, and AI training that don't require prior experience. Just be honest about your background in your application — we'll match you to roles that fit.

How does the verification process work?

Once you're matched with a position, we'll send you a link to verify your identity through a secure third-party platform. This is standard practice to protect both you and the employer. The process typically takes 10-15 minutes.

I'm a student — can I still apply?

Absolutely. Many of our roles are a great fit for students — especially part-time positions in data entry, AI training, and customer support. You can work around your class schedule and build real professional experience. During the application, we'll ask for your school name and email to confirm you're a student.

Can I work part-time?

Yes. Many of our positions offer flexible, part-time schedules — perfect for students or anyone balancing other commitments. You can indicate your availability preference in your application. Whether you're looking for a few hours a week or a full-time commitment, we have options for you.
