Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free to join?

Yes, 100%. RemoteEdge never charges workers any fees. We're compensated by the companies when we successfully match them with qualified candidates. You'll never be asked for payment information.

How long does the matching process take?

Most applicants hear back within 2–3 business days. Our team carefully reviews each application to find the best fit. Some high-demand roles may match even faster.

What types of remote jobs are available?

We offer a wide range of remote positions including AI Training & Data Annotation, Virtual Assistant, Customer Support, Content & Writing, Sales & Marketing, and Data Entry & Research. Pay ranges from $15 to $75+ per hour depending on the role and experience level.

Do I need prior experience?

Not necessarily. While some roles require specific skills, many of our positions are open to beginners. We have entry-level roles like data entry and basic customer support that welcome people new to remote work. Just be honest about your experience level in your application.

How does the verification process work?

Once you're matched with a position, we'll send you a link to complete identity verification (KYC) through a secure third-party platform. This is a standard industry practice to protect both you and the employer. The process typically takes 10–15 minutes.

Can I work part-time?

Absolutely. Many of our positions offer flexible, part-time schedules. You can indicate your availability preference in your application. Whether you're looking for a few hours a week or a full-time commitment, we have options for you.
